# Moderation playbook (population-level signals only)

Use these as **community moderation** options only. They are not medical or clinical advice.

## Stable

- Continue routine moderation and keep general community resources visible in the sidebar or wiki.

## Early Vulnerability Signal

- Increase routine visibility of pinned resources and community guidelines; monitor aggregate posting patterns without targeting individuals.

## Elevated Distress

- Consider a pinned thread for community resources and peer support; brief moderator awareness of elevated aggregate activity (no individual outreach based solely on this signal).

## Severe Community Distress Signal

- Escalate to senior moderators for community-level response; prominently post official subreddit rules and static mental health resource links; consider temporary community announcements (aggregate signal only).
